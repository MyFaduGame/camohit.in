A practicing
CA. A real advisor.

I am CA Mohit Sharma, a qualified Chartered Accountant in active practice at Ahmedabad, Gujarat. Through Mohit B Sharma & Associates, I serve MSMEs, corporates, startups, and individuals across India — from income tax and GST compliance to bank finance, audits, and business advisory.

My foundation in finance runs deeper than my qualification. My father, Basant Sharma, has been a practicing tax consultant for 22+ years with a practice of 1,500+ clients.
